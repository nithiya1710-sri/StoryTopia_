Chapter 1: Snow White

Snow White is a kind princess with skin as white as snow. The Evil Queen, jealous of her beauty, orders a huntsman to kill her. Instead, he lets her escape into the forest.

Chapter 2: The Seven Dwarfs

Snow White finds a cottage belonging to seven dwarfs who take care of her. Meanwhile, the Evil Queen plots to kill her using dark magic.

Chapter 3: The Awakening

The Queen poisons Snow White. She falls into a deep sleep until a prince awakens her with a kiss. They live happily ever after.
