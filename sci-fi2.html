Chapter 1: Mysterious Signal

In 2150, Captain Rhea Carson leads a mission aboard the spaceship Stellar Quest to investigate a strange signal from a distant planet. The crew, excited by the discovery, prepares for landing. However, the planet is cloaked in mystery, with no previous records of its existence. As they approach, strange anomalies affect the ship's systems. Uncertain and curious, Rhea decides to explore, unaware of the dangers ahead.

It was a place where stories were born, and every being had a tale to tell. The first tale we share is about a humble village, nestled between the mountains.

Chapter 2: Lost in Shadows

Landing on the planet, the crew finds a deserted alien city, eerily silent and abandoned. As they explore, they uncover ancient technology, but something feels off. Sudden power failures and disorienting visions plague the crew, and soon they realize they’re not alone. Invisible forces begin manipulating their minds, creating paranoia. Rhea suspects the signal was a trap, but it’s too late—their exit route is cut off.

The journey led Arin through enchanted forests, over rolling hills, and across wide rivers. Yet, the greatest challenge was still ahead.

Chapter 3: Escape or Extinction

With their ship disabled and tensions rising, Rhea uncovers the planet’s secret: a sentient alien entity controlling the environment. It feeds off fear and confusion, using illusions to trap explorers. Desperate, Rhea hacks the alien tech to disrupt the entity’s control. As the planet shakes violently, they race back to the ship. Escaping just in time, the crew watches the planet vanish, realizing they were on the edge of oblivion.
