Chapter 1: Beauty and the Beast

Belle, a kind young woman, lives with her father, Maurice, in a small village. Maurice gets lost and captured by the Beast, a cursed prince. Belle offers to take her father’s place as the Beast’s prisoner. Though initially scared, Belle begins to see the Beast’s kind side with the help of the enchanted servants. A bond slowly forms between them, despite their difficult start.

It was a place where stories were born, and every being had a tale to tell. The first tale we share is about a humble village, nestled between the mountains.

Chapter 2: True Friendship

Belle and the Beast grow closer, spending time together and sharing stories. The Beast falls in love with Belle but doubts she can love him in return. When Belle sees her father in danger through a magical mirror, the Beast lets her go, even though it might mean he remains cursed forever. Back in the village, Gaston manipulates the townspeople into attacking the Beast’s castle.

The journey led Arin through enchanted forests, over rolling hills, and across wide rivers. Yet, the greatest challenge was still ahead.

Chapter 3: Love's Power

Gaston leads the villagers to the Beast’s castle, but Belle escapes to warn him. A battle ensues, and the Beast is mortally wounded. As he lays dying, Belle confesses her love for him, breaking the curse. The Beast transforms back into a prince, and the castle’s inhabitants are freed. Belle and the prince share a joyful reunion, proving that true beauty lies within, and love conquers all.
